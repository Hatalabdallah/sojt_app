Ask our customer support

What is the Structured On-the-Job Training (SOJT) program?

Our SOJT program is designed to empower individuals and organizations by providing hands-on, real-world experience tailored to specific job roles, enhancing learning and performance.

How do I enroll in the SOJT program?

You can sign up for our SOJT program with an upfront payment of £300 for the first two months. After that, a subscription fee of £100 per month applies. Visit our website or contact us for more details.

What career paths do you offer training for?

We specialize in training for technology and data-driven careers, including roles in DevOps, Cloud Engineering, Data Engineering, AI, and Security & Networking.

What is the success rate of your program?

Our program boasts an 80% completion rate, with alumni experiencing an average annual wage gain of £24,000 after completing the program.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods for the program fees, including credit/debit cards and bank transfers. Please contact us for specific options.

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy allows for a full refund within the first 14 days of enrollment. After that period, refunds are not available. Please refer to our terms for more details.

How does the mentorship work in the SOJT program?

Our program includes expert mentorship from experienced professionals who provide guidance, support, and continuous feedback to help learners refine their skills and accelerate their career growth.

What types of assignments can I expect?

Participants will engage in agile work-based assignments that emphasize collaboration, flexibility, and continuous improvement, providing hands-on experience in real-world scenarios.

How long does the SOJT program last?

The duration of the SOJT program varies based on the specific learning plan and individual progress, but typically spans several months to ensure comprehensive skill development.

Can I switch my learning path during the program?

Yes, we offer flexibility in our SOJT program, allowing participants to switch their learning paths based on their evolving interests and career goals.
